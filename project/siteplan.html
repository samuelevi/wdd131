Website Site Plan

A comprehensive plan for the GreenThumb Garden Club website

Site Name

GreenThumb Garden Club

I chose the name "GreenThumb Garden Club" because it clearly communicates the website's purpose while being memorable and approachable. The term "green thumb" is a well-known idiom associated with gardening success, making it instantly recognizable to our target audience.

Suggested Domain Name: greenthumbgarden.club

Site Purpose

The GreenThumb Garden Club website serves as a central hub for local gardening enthusiasts of all skill levels. Our primary goals are to provide valuable gardening resources, foster community engagement, and organize local events. The site will feature seasonal planting guides tailored to our region, pest control tips using organic methods, and information about upcoming club meetings and plant swap events.

By consolidating these resources in one accessible location, we aim to create a supportive and knowledgeable community that helps both beginners and experienced gardeners succeed in their gardening endeavors.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Beginner Gardener Seeking Guidance

User Question: "What are the best beginner-friendly vegetables to plant in my area this spring, and how do I prepare my soil?"

A new gardener in the area has never grown vegetables before and wants to start with spring planting. They need practical, region-specific advice on which vegetables are easiest to grow and how to prepare their garden bed. This scenario drives the need for seasonal planting guides and beginner-friendly resources on our site.

Scenario 2: Experienced Gardener Seeking Community Help

User Question: "My tomato plants have strange spots on their leaves. Has anyone in the club experienced this, and what organic pest control methods worked for them?"

An experienced gardener is facing a pest or disease problem and wants to tap into the collective knowledge of the club. They prefer organic solutions and value recommendations from other local gardeners who understand the regional climate and conditions. This scenario highlights the importance of our community forum and pest control resource section.

Scenario 3: Member Looking for Events

User Question: "When is the next GreenThumb Garden Club meeting or plant swap event?"

A club member wants to stay connected with the community by attending upcoming events. They need easy access to a calendar or events page that clearly displays meeting dates, times, locations, and event details. This scenario emphasizes the need for an easily accessible events section on the homepage.

Color Schema

I selected a color palette inspired by nature to reflect the gardening theme of the website. The colors work together to create a cohesive, welcoming, and professional appearance while maintaining excellent readability and accessibility.

Primary Green

Hex Code: #4CAF50

Usage: Headings (H1, H2), buttons, important accents, and navigation elements

This vibrant green represents growth, nature, and freshness. It will be the dominant color that users associate with the GreenThumb brand.

Earthy Brown

Hex Code: #795548

Usage: Body text, subheadings (H3, H4), borders, and secondary accents

This warm brown symbolizes soil, stability, and natural elements. It provides a grounding color that pairs beautifully with the primary green.

Light Cream

Hex Code: #F5F5DC

Usage: Background color for main content areas and overall page background

This soft, neutral cream provides a clean, calming backdrop that allows the other colors to stand out without causing eye strain.

Typography

I selected a combination of three fonts to ensure readability, visual hierarchy, and a friendly, approachable feel. These fonts are all available through Google Fonts and are optimized for web use.

Usage: All headings (H1, H2, H3, H4, H5, H6)

Montserrat is a clean, modern, and highly readable sans-serif font. It gives the site a contemporary and inviting look, making headings stand out clearly.

Open Sans - Readable & Friendly

Aa Bb Cc Dd Ee Ff Gg Hh Ii Jj Kk Ll Mm Nn Oo Pp Qq Rr Ss Tt Uu Vv Ww Xx Yy Zz 0123456789

Usage: Body text, paragraphs, lists, and general content

Open Sans is a highly legible sans-serif font that pairs well with Montserrat. It ensures that the main content is easy to read on all devices and screen sizes.

Lora - Elegant & Distinctive

Aa Bb Cc Dd Ee Ff Gg Hh Ii Jj Kk Ll Mm Nn Oo Pp Qq Rr Ss Tt Uu Vv Ww Xx Yy Zz 0123456789

Usage: Blockquotes, captions, and special emphasis elements

Lora is a classic serif font that adds elegance and contrast. It will be used sparingly for special elements to create visual interest without overwhelming the design.
